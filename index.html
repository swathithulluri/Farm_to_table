About Us

  • As our nation’s urban areas grow, consumers in the grocery store are increasingly separated from the producers who grow the meat, fruit and vegetables they buy there.
  • While consumers are sometimes able to purchase food directly from the farmers that produce it, the majority of food sold in the India comes from grocery stores.
  • “Farm-to-table” is a growing social movement that promotes serving locally grown food to consumers. But it’s not just about proximity. The movement is also driven by an emphasis on food safety, freshness , Transparency and the ability to track where your food came from.
